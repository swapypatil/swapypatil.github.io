SafeDriveDroid
Drive-Mode Application for Road Safety
MAR 2018 - APR 2018

SafeDriveDroid is a simple and easy to use Android app that blocks all the incoming calls and messages when the driver is driving a vehicle. The drive mode is auto enabled when the speed of the vehicle increases above a threshold level say 20 km/hr. The drive mode can also be enabled manually.


The app detects if the driver met with an accident and also sends the driver's current location to his 3 priority contacts(Ambulance contact can be added as priority contact for safety). The messages and calls that were blocked are shown to the driver after the drive mode is disabled.


The app also sends auto-replies to the callers. In case of emergency calls, the app notifies the driver about emergency call using voiceover. The app also notifies the driver using voiceover and parent with message if the speed limit is exceeded than the speed limit specified using parental control.


The app is available in English as well as provides support to 2 native languages (Hindi, Marathi). This app won 1st prize at the Software Edition of Smart India Hackathon 2018.
